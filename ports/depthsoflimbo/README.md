## Controls

Two sticks:
| Button | Action |
|--|--|
|DPAD/LSTICK|Movement|
|RSTICK|Aim|
|R1/L1|Shoot|
|A|Confirm|
|B|Use active/back|
|X|Interact/drop|
|Y|Change weapon|
|Start|Pause|
|Select|Map|

One and zero sticks:
| Button | Action |
|--|--|
|DPAD or LSTICK|Movement|
|A|Shoot right|
|B|Shoot down|
|X|Shoot up|
|Y|Shoot left|
|SELECT+A or R2|Confirm|
|SELECT+B or L2|Back/Pause|
|R1|Interact/drop|
|L1|Change weapon|
|Start|Use active|
|Select|Map|
